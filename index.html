Webservice currently not reachable

Helmholtz Munich has been the target of a cyberattack, which our IT detected on March 15 and responded with immediate defensive measures. According to the investigating police authorities, the attack is part of a current wave of attacks. In this context, Helmholtz Munich immediately called in designated external specialists, preventively shut down all relevant systems, started the forensic investigations, informed the authorities about the incident and set up a crisis team with various task forces.

Due to this situation, Omicscience.com is not reachable at the moment. We apologize for the inconvenience and are working hard to get this service back online as soon as possible.
